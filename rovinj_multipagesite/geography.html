Geography

Rovinj is right on the coast of the Istrian peninsula, hence why they have such amazing fresh seafood. Rovinj was originally an island, but when the channel that had separated it from the mainland was filled, it triggered the start of what Rovinj is today.

It is built on a hilly terrain and has cobblestone walls and streets giving it that lovely old-town feel. Rovinj isn't known for sandy beaches, as the costal area is quite rocky, however the rocky beaches they have are stunning and the water is beautiful.
