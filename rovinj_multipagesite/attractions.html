Attractions

Visit parks in the area

You can hop on a tour boat rom the port, or travel by car to visit Lim Fjord. A beautiful park where you can hike and enjoy the scenery. Check out the stunning river canal as you walk through the wooded trails. If your hungry after your hike, stop and take a gander at the restaurant at the head of the river, and enjoy some fresh seafood!

If you want to just take a swim and enjoy the water, there are tons of local beaches. Cisterna Bay has many stunning areas to swim with clear water and pebble beaches. You can travel by car, as it is only about 6 km outside of Rovinj. If you want somewhere a bit closer to the center, Monte beach is another cute little rocky beach.

Would you rather spend some time exploring the history and architecture of the area? The St. Euphemia Cathedral is a stunning. Built in the 17th century by the Venetians who were in control of Rovinj during this time, this Cathedral contains some beautiful historical architecture, and when you reach the top, you can look out on the lovely views. Dvigrad is another interesting historical site, an old medieval town abandoned in the 1700's, where a plague ran rampant.

There is an extensive list of things to explore in Croatia. If your feeling like enjoying the beautiful water you can check out the port and walk the deck with a gelato (it's a great place to watch the sun set), or you can set out for something more adventerous. Rovinj is a great vacation spot for all travellers.
