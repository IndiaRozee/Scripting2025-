Economy

Tourism

Rovinj economy is greatly impacted by tourism as it was always a forgotten gem, but in recent years has gained popularity. With Rovinj being one of the Croatian regions with the most overnight stays I'm sure you can understand why tourism is such a pillar in their economy.

Fishing

As Rovinj is right on the coast fishing is a huge part of Rovinj's economy. Fishing provides jobs and food for many and is also the reason for their bountiful seafood cuisine!

Exchanging of goods and agriculture

Farming, although it was never a huge branch of the economy in Rovinj, has had a recent incline in people using agriculture, horticulture, and viticulture to fund their lifestyles. The exchange of goods and services in the community also contributes largely to the economy, especially when it comes to artisans and food places due to tourism being at an all time high.
