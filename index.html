About!

Hi! My name is India and I'm a first-year student at the Lethbridge Polytechnic in the Multimedia Production course. I love most things creative, but my interests lean towards design and animation.

I am a 19-year-old artist who is just starting out in building her career. I was born and raised in Lethbridge Alberta and stuck around to complete my studies. When I started to take interest in art, I was about 8 and got sucked into the work of cartoons and comics. It wasn't until after I tried to follow through with getting a degree in journalism that I realized that I wanted to begin a career that utilized that skill. I have just started my first year in multimedia production and have found it to help me broaden my horizons as I have now found an interest in web design and coding. This versatility of this program is what has sucked me in, and I hope to learn and grow in each aspect of media design while making connections and investing in my career!

I want to see more!

Interests!

My main focuses currently are to grow in graphics, animation, and character design, although I enjoy some recreational photography on the side. I love the idea of taking a story and turning it into art, and that takes thought, time, and attention to detail. The style of animation or details on specific characters can make all the difference when watching an animation or playing a game. Growing up with a love for many characters from cartoons, games or comics has made me want to create nostalgic characters like that for future generation. Web design and coding is another more recent interest that I'm not very well versed in, but aim to learn and grow in.

Portfolio!

Although I don't yet have much experince in animation or graphic design I hope to gain more through the Multimedia program. However my love for sketching and character design is what brought me here!

Coding and web-design are not aspects of media arts as much as I do. It has been so cool learning about it, and I plan to get more into it.

All in all I have not yet built up my portfolio, but in the next few years as I gain skills and experinces, I hope to build upon it.

Values!

All in all, I just want to make things that don't only appeal to the eye but also help to build a story. I want people to see my work and see the effort that went into it. I'm a professional person, but I also like to mess around and be playful, and I hope for that to be evident in my work, by bringing joy into all of the things I create.
